Privacy

ARMY RECALL may allow users to enter basic contact information, such as names and phone numbers. This information is stored locally on the user's device and is not sent to ARMY RECALL servers.

Information Stored on Device

Users may choose to enter names and phone numbers inside the app. This information is saved locally on the user's device for app functionality and convenience.

No Server Storage

ARMY RECALL does not store names or phone numbers on company servers. The information remains on the user's device unless the user chooses to share it outside the app.

Security

Do not enter classified information, mission details, unit-sensitive data, or other sensitive operational information into ARMY RECALL.
